Step into the charm of 4302 37th Street NW, a delightful semi-detached home nestled on a serene, tree-lined street in the heart of North Cleveland Park. As you arrive, you'll be greeted by a charming slate walkway leading to a welcoming front porch—perfect for sipping your morning coffee! Inside, the main level unfolds into a spacious living area, accentuated by a cozy wood-burning fireplace that creates an inviting atmosphere. The kitchen flows seamlessly into a cheerful back room adorned with skylights, ideal for a breakfast nook, play space, or additional sitting area. The first floor is rounded out by a convenient half-bath as well as ingenious foyer closet.

Venture outside to discover a stunning screened porch with flooring crafted from Ipe wood, an excellent spot for relaxation or entertaining, all while overlooking a picturesque backyard brimming with lush plants and beautiful stonework. Upstairs, three sunlit bedrooms await, including a primary suite with its own bathroom and a spacious back bedroom with access to a large private deck—a perfect retreat!

The lower level boasts a sizable unfinished area, ripe with potential to be transformed into a fun recreation room, along with two additional storage rooms, a half-bath, and a laundry area. You'll appreciate the convenience of attic storage and a one-car garage, which is ideal for small car parking or storing bikes and gardening tools.
